TRANSPORTATION

  1. B U S E S

    Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML) operates an extensive bus network within the city and its suburbs. These buses are the most commonly used public transport, offering affordable and accessible commuting options. The BRTS (Bus Rapid Transit System) provides dedicated lanes for buses, ensuring faster and more efficient travel on certain routes.

  2. M E T R O

    The Pune Metro is a major urban mass transit project designed to address the growing transportation needs of Pune, a rapidly expanding city in Maharashtra, India. The metro aims to provide an efficient, reliable, and environmentally friendly mode of public transport to ease traffic congestion and improve the overall quality of life for Pune's residents. The Pune Metro project is being developed by Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited (Maha-Metro), a joint venture between the Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra. The project was officially inaugurated in December 2016, and it is being implemented in phases.

  3. R A I L W A Y S

    Pune Railway Station is a major hub connecting the city to various parts of India. It is an important junction for long-distance trains, catering to both passenger and freight traffic. Additionally, the city has suburban railway services that connect Pune to nearby areas like Lonavala and Talegaon.

  4. RICKSHAWS & TAXIS

    Auto rickshaws are a popular mode of transport for short distances. They are easily available and provide a convenient way to navigate through the city's traffic. Traditional taxis and app-based ride-sharing services like Ola and Uber are widely used in Pune. These services offer comfort and convenience, especially for longer distances or airport transfers.
