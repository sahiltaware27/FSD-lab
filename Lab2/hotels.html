HOTELS

  1. THE WESTIN, PUNE

    The Westin Pune Koregaon Park is renowned for its luxurious amenities and exceptional service. The hotel offers elegant rooms with modern furnishings, a large outdoor pool, and a well-equipped fitness center. The on-site restaurants serve a variety of cuisines, including Indian, Asian, and international dishes. The hotel’s spa provides a range of treatments for relaxation and rejuvenation.

  2. CONRAD, PUNE

    Conrad Pune is a luxurious property that offers opulent accommodations with elegant design elements. The hotel features a world-class spa, an outdoor pool, and several fine dining options, including international and local cuisines. The rooms and suites are designed with comfort and style in mind, offering a luxurious retreat in the heart of Pune.

  3. JW MARRIOTT

    JW Marriott Pune is a five-star luxury hotel renowned for its sophisticated style and exceptional service. Located in the heart of the city, the hotel provides a blend of modern comfort and elegant decor. It’s popular among both business and leisure travelers for its high standards of hospitality and extensive range of amenities.

  4. HYATT, PUNE

    Hyatt Pune combines modern comfort with high-quality service. It features spacious rooms with contemporary decor, a large outdoor swimming pool, and a state-of-the-art fitness center. The hotel’s dining options include a multi-cuisine restaurant, a cafe, and a bar. The spa offers a range of treatments and massages.

  5. SHERATON GRAND, PUNE

    Sheraton Grand is a premium five-star hotel located in Bund Garden, Pune. It is renowned for its upscale amenities, sophisticated design, and exceptional service, catering to both business and leisure travelers. The hotel combines modern luxury with warm hospitality, making it a popular choice for guests seeking a high-end experience in Pune.
